AIONEX Digital Systems

Marketing site for AIONEX — Digital Systems & Automation, positioned for the German market. The experience presents the brand as a credible technology partner: clear services story, modern visual language, and performance suited to B2B discovery and contact.

React Next.js TypeScript Vercel Responsive
Live site GitHub

Overview

AIONEX focuses on digital systems and automation. The website’s role is to translate that positioning into a concise, confident web presence: who the brand is, what problems it addresses, and how prospects can engage—without noise or generic template feel.

The live deployment at corex-website-g4ba.vercel.app showcases the public-facing experience end users see in production.

Problem

Technical and automation consultancies often sound interchangeable online. AIONEX needed a site that signals depth in systems and automation while remaining approachable for German-speaking business audiences scanning for partners.

The site had to load quickly, work flawlessly on mobile, and support future content updates (services, proof points, contact flows) without rebuilding layout from scratch.

Solution

A Next.js–based frontend with component-driven sections: hero, value proposition, capabilities aligned with digital systems and automation, and strong calls-to-action. Typography, spacing, and motion (where used) support a premium B2B aesthetic.

Hosting on Vercel gives edge delivery, preview deployments for iterations, and a straightforward path from commit to production—aligned with how modern marketing sites for tech brands are shipped.

Architecture

Visitors interact with a client-rendered or hybrid Next.js application; assets and pages are served through Vercel’s network for low latency in Europe.
